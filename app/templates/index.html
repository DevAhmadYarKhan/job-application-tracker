Track Every Application in One Place

Job Application Tracker helps you organize applications, monitor deadlines, and keep your job search clear and structured.

What It Does

The website is designed to store your job applications in one place so you can quickly see where you have applied, what stage each application is in, and which deadlines are coming up.

How It Works

You create and manage application entries, then update them as your progress changes. Instead of tracking everything manually, the platform is intended to give you a single overview of your search.

Why Use It

Keeping application details organized can reduce missed deadlines, repeated effort, and confusion during a busy job hunt. This tracker is meant to make that process simpler and easier to manage.
